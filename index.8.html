About:

David Guetta is a French DJ, music producer, and songwriter who has become one of the most successful electronic dance music (EDM) artists of all time. He was born on November 7, 1967, in Paris, France. Guetta started his career as a DJ in the Paris club scene in the 1980s and 1990s, and he released his first album, "Just a Little More Love," in 2002..

Work:

David Guetta is a multi-talented artist who has made a significant impact on the electronic dance music (EDM) industry. Throughout his career, Guetta has worked on various projects as a DJ, music producer, and songwriter.

Resume:

Guetta is widely recognized for his contributions to the electronic dance music (EDM) genre and is considered one of the pioneers of the genre. He has produced and collaborated on numerous hit songs and has been awarded several Grammy Awards for his work
